A brilliant flute artist Dr. B.L Sharma from Jaipur (Rajasthan) is fortunate enough to be from a family of musicians. Initially, Dr. B.L Sharma started learning music from his father late Shri Devki Nandan Sharma, Who was a renowned singer for DHRUPAD & DHAMAR and was disciple of late Ustad Karamat Khan of Jaipur. Later On, he got a short guidance from Padamshri Vijay Rao and Shri Hari Prasad Chauresia(Padam Bhusan), the great flute artist of the country.

Dr.B.L Sharma renders khayal(Gayaki-Style) and light composition(Dhun) with equal mastry as well as folk music too. He is a regular performer through all India Radio since 1969.Dr.B.L Sharma has given performances throughout the country, specially in All India Music Conferences held at Jaipur, Mumbay, Calcutta, Allahbad, Indore, Ahmedabad, Bhuj, Ajmer and Rajkot etc. and amongst those teh mentionable performance was from Tarun Kalakar Samaroh, Jaipur, Kal Ke Kalakar Sangeet Sammelan, Sangeet Natak Aceademy Festival, Jaipur, Swami Hari Das Sangeet Samaroh, Mumbay, All India Music Conference at Ajmer and Jawahar Kala Kendra, Jaipur.
